About

    Hey, I'm Nicholas Rich and welcome to my static space. I love reading, thinking about random stuff and dabbling with diverse subjects. I will be taking mental dumps in my "blog" section and will be listing the books I have read in the "bookshelf" session.

    The lockdown period has been an inflection point in my life. I was once plagued with existential crisis but have discovered three factors that has breathed meaning to life. Firstly, the importance of knowledge acquisition. Secondly, developing a multidisciplinary mind and lastly, having a child-like curiosity. The 3rd factor kinda covers the first 2 :p hahah

    I have been spending the past few months reading more extensively and intensively than ever before and I hope you will be inspired to do the same and make it a moral obligation to compound your knowledge daily.

    I have used wordpress and blogspot in the past to digitally pen down my ideas. However, coding a website has definitely been pretty damn fun.(Coders please don't mock my newbie skills :( ) Anyways, hope you find some utility here.
